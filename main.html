Why Hire a Developer?

The majority of mobile website visitors leave if it takes longer than 3 seconds to load. As such, platforms like Squarespace, WordPress, or Wix could go from an appealing solution for quickly getting a website up and running to something that could be costing you and your business thousands of clients as they close the tab before your content even loads.

That's not to say that all websites built using those tools are slow, just that template-built sites don't match the benefits of custom-made sites. I believe in making fast, accessible, and lightweight applications. That means no unnecessary libraries, fonts, scripts, or other page blocking content increases the wait time between a user clicking on your website and the content showing on their screen.

Search Engine Optimization

SEO is another area where web-building platforms suffer. Not having total control over the source code means potentially losing on SEO performance gains that would be trivial for a Web Developer to implement on your website but incredibly difficult for platforms like those to do.
